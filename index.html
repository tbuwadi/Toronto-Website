───── ABOUT ─────

Toronto is the capital city of Ontario, and home to around 3 million people making it the country's most populous city. This multiculutral city is one of the most diverse cities in the world. This cosmopolitan city is home to the CN Tower, and the Roger's Center. Nearby is another Canadian landmark, Niagra Falls. Leading the world in areas such as finance and technology, Toronto is a Canadian treasure. The largest industries in Canada are Real Estate and Financial Services. Wholesale and Retail Trade are also some large Toronto industries. Toronto is one of Canada's most well- known cities for its tourism indsutry.

In Toronto, the most common demographics in order are White, East Asian, South Asian, Black, Southeast Asian, Latin American, West Asian, Arab, and Aborginal with 1.3% other races. The area of Toronto is 630 km2 (243 sq mi), and the population density is 4,149.5 people per square kilometer (10,750/sq mi).

Toronto contributes 2.93 million people to Canada's population of 37.59 million. Toronto is in the Continent of North America and geography wise it is bound by Lake Ontario, Etobicoke Creek, and Highway 427. The climate in Toronto is severe winters with snow, and hot summers. Lake Ontario helps to keep the summer cool and the winter warmer.

Poutine

A delicious mix of fries and gravy topped with cheese curds. This popular fast food among Canadians can be topped with bacon.

Peameal Bacon

This food is a fine meat made from trimmed, lean pork rolled in cornmeal. This breakfast meat is a Southern Ontario favourite.

Nanaimo Bar

Named after Nanaimo, British Columbia, this no-bake bar is a desert consisting of a crumb base, with custard and chocolate.

Butter Tart

This treat consists of a sweet filling baked in a pastry shell. Different nuts or raisins can be added for an extra zang of flavour.
